ESP GYM

Welcome to the world's first telepathy training gym!

ESP GYM offers a structured way to explore telepathy and psi sensitivity through interactive training, immediate feedback, and thoughtful self-observation. Whether you are curious, skeptical, or experienced, these tools let you test, refine, and track your own extrasensory perception.

ESP GYM can be installed as an app on many modern devices, including Windows PCs, iPhone and iPad, Android phones and tablets, and other devices with current browsers that support app installation.

What Is ESP GYM?

ESP GYM is a growing environment for training and exploring human extrasensory perception. It combines structured exercises, immediate feedback, performance reporting, and tools designed to support disciplined personal experimentation.

The goal is not merely to inspire belief, but to make careful practice possible, whether you work with a partner or, in some cases, alone.

Experiences

Telepathy Beginner

A structured sender-receiver app for practicing visual telepathy and tracking performance over time.

ESP PRO

Advanced features for deeper experimentation, richer reporting, and broader tools for serious practice.

Ongoing Research

Individuals who demonstrate talent in Telepathy Beginner are encouraged to participate in the ongoing research program.

Learning Center

What Is Telepathy?

A concise introduction to the telepathic transfer of visual information and why structured practice may help subtle impressions become easier to notice.

Telepathy Training

Graded telepathy exercises with multiple levels of difficulty. You and a partner can take turns developing both receiving and transmitting skills.

Performance Reports and Visualization

Reporting, graphing, and interpretation tools help users see whether practice is producing improvement or merely reflecting chance performance.

Guided ESP Development

ESP GYM also includes educational material, explanatory frameworks, and techniques that can help users understand and strengthen psi sensitivity.

Experimental and Research-Oriented Tools

ESP GYM is built to support careful personal experimentation. It also supports formal data collection programs.

Why Practice Matters

Like many human abilities, ESP may be influenced by attention, trust, consistency, and experience. Repeated, structured practice may help some people become more sensitive to subtle impressions that otherwise go unnoticed. Whether the effect is large, small, stable, or elusive, disciplined practice creates the opportunity to observe it more clearly and evaluate it more honestly.

Special Participation

From time to time, ESP GYM may invite selected individuals into early-access programs, founder groups, special experiments, or guided experiences. If you have received an invitation code, you can use it above to gain user access to ESP PRO.

    About Telepathy Beginner

    Telepathy Beginner is an ESP Gym practice tool designed to help two people explore sender-receiver style telepathy exercises in a structured way. The app supports progressive difficulty levels, records practice history for review, and is intended to make repeated telepathy training sessions easy to run.

    The goal of the app is training and experimentation. It is not presented as a medical, psychological, or scientific diagnostic instrument. Users should treat it as a practice environment for focused attention, perception, and partner-based ESP exercises.

    Privacy Policy

    ESP Gym stores only the information needed to operate the app and preserve practice history. This may include names entered by users, trial results, timestamps, difficulty levels, and location information when location access is enabled for distance or reporting features.

    Practice history may be stored on the ESP Gym server so users can review reports later and so administrators can manage the system. ESP Gym does not sell user data or intentionally share it for advertising purposes. Administrative access is limited to maintaining the service, reviewing technical status, and supporting users.

    If you send a message through the contact form, the contents of that message will be delivered to ESP Gym by email so your question or feedback can be reviewed. Please do not include sensitive financial, medical, or other highly confidential information in app messages.
