Background information about Telepathy Beginner and how ESP Gym handles your data.

About Telepathy Beginner

Telepathy Beginner is an ESP Gym practice tool designed to help two people explore sender-receiver style telepathy exercises in a structured way. The app supports progressive difficulty levels, records practice history for review, and is intended to make repeated telepathy training sessions easy to run.

The goal of the app is training and experimentation. It is not presented as a medical, psychological, or scientific diagnostic instrument. Users should treat it as a practice environment for focused attention, perception, and partner-based ESP exercises.

Privacy Policy

ESP Gym stores only the information needed to operate the app and preserve practice history. This may include names entered by users, trial results, timestamps, difficulty levels, and location information when location access is enabled for distance or reporting features.

Practice history may be stored on the ESP Gym server so users can review reports later and so administrators can manage the system. ESP Gym does not sell user data or intentionally share it for advertising purposes. Administrative access is limited to maintaining the service, reviewing technical status, and supporting users.

If you send a message through the contact form, the contents of that message will be delivered to ESP Gym by email so your question or feedback can be reviewed. Please do not include sensitive financial, medical, or other highly confidential information in app messages.
