[indent]By this point you have filled out the Questionnaire After First Live Telepathy Practice.[/indent]

[indent]The questions ask you to intospect into your own information processing system. One important action that can be worked on is to give up trying to name something but rather, to describe it.[/indent]

[indent]Is it straight?    Not straight?    Round?    Boxy?    Located to the left side      to the right side      is it big        any color?    Rough?      Smooth?        what texture?      Outside?      Inside?      Nature?     Cityscape?[/indent]

[indent]A few trials are not enough to scientifically verify whether you have telepathic ability or not. Even if you got many of the trials correct, this could be the result of pure chance alone.[/indent]

[indent]However, if you saw anything after the beep, when an image was displayed to the sender, you know what you saw. If the right number of blobs appered in your mind’s eye at the right time – either one possibly orange blob, or more than one possibly orange blob – even on just one trial, this is supportive of the conclusion that you actually received some information telepathically.  But, you may have been generating what you saw from your own mind alone. Yet, if they appeared at the right time and even may have surprised you a bit, then it is less likely that you were generating them from your own mind.[/indent]

[indent]Go ahead, being in denial is the easiest route to take at this point. More practice is called for. More data. In karate training, some people improve steadily right from the beginning and get their black belt, while others stay low for a long time and then improve rapidly and get their black belt. If you are curious as to how you are doing, after perhaps twenty trials, see what the statistics tell you about whether you are performing at chance level or better than chance.[/indent[

TO PONDER BEFORE THE NEXT LIVE SESSION:  Where is your “mind’s eye” ?

[indent]If what is meant by “you” is that part of yourself which is aware, can you become aware of that part? Is that where your awareness focuses when attempting to view the contents of your mind’s eye?[/indent]

[indent]“Awareness” is an amazing concept – highly related to “consciousness” – another amazing concept. The real question is, Where is it?  What are its boundaries? What is it in touch with?[/indent]

[indent]This last question relates to telepathy and all ESP abilities. Certainly on the one hand it is in touch with our brain’s information processing system. But in order to explain the ability to read data in the Akashic record, as shown by the case studies brought up in Lesson x, and possibly as also demonstrated in your own experience with telepathic perception, something else other than the immediate physical sensory environment is making information available in real time.[/indent]

[indent]Our awareness, then, is in touch with another domain of reality which appears to be totally connected and store information about everything. In what format is that information? What we do when we view the contents of our mind’s eye is perform a translation of that other-domain information into information that can be processed by the brain – for example, words, ideas, visual images, sounds, feelings, smells.[/indent]

[indent]If we assume that it is a single information structure that encodes the Akashic record, then all things, objects, thoughts, events, - at all scales and all times – are expressed by that same information. Then the outwardly different ESP abilities, telepathy, clairvoyance, precognition are all accessing that same fountain. A thought and an object are represented by the same format /  encoding of information. That is the information we strive to translate into its visual features – and we can do it. And it apparently can work in the reverse direction – as evidenced by PK ability, where the mind can influence matter.[/indent]
